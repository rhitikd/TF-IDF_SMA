TF-IDF stands for "Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency" and is a numerical representation of the importance of a term in a document within a collection of documents. It is commonly used in natural language processing (NLP) and information retrieval tasks, such as text classification, information retrieval, and text summarization.
